How it works

HRDirect FRD is designed to help mid-size and large companies manage their employees more efficiently. We all know the importance of Human Ressource management. Indeed, a company is defined not only by its economic capital and technical skills (know-how), but also by its human capital. Employee interaction and motivation are success factors that should not be overlooked for the smooth running of a company. This is where human resources managers will be involved. Their function is multifaceted: it covers recruitment, orientation, staff training, remuneration, benefits and communication. HR has a strategic role and is one of the main actors in meeting the company's challenges. Good human resources management allows companies to gain an advantage over their competitors and that is where HRDirect FRD comes into play.

But how does it work ?

HRDirect FRD is based on Google's FireBase Realtime DataBase technology. It allows Human resource managers to interact with each other in real time and way more efficiently. Every HR managers will get an acces to a Firebase Realtime Database, allowing them to consult, create, modify or delete datas. Being a realtime technology, by doing so, each one of them will observe in real time the changes made and will thus, always have updated datas.

The main goal being for them to fill in the maximum of information about their employees. Gender, race, age, main skills or even global appreciation, everything is possible, as long as every managers follow the same layouts. It will thus allow HR managers to have constant up-to-date information about all the employees in the firm and thus greatly simply human ressource management. An employee suddently left the firm and need to be replaced urgently ? Managers will have acces to employees having the same skills and their appreciation, making it easier to replace him or to facilitate internal promotion. There is an upcoming conference to be given by the company in a certain city but the branch office does not have any spanish speaker employee ? No problem, with HRDirect FRD managers can easily look within the firm for technically qualified spanish speaker employees.

    We will start of course by adding information about every employees using the same layout. To do so, each manager will convert their existing excel file where they have datas of all their employees into JSON file.   • Now, managers will upload (using fonction .set in script.js) their JSON data into Firebase
  • By doing so, they have now acces to the datas of every single one employee within the firm
  • Any change, will automatically update the firebase and the datas for everyone so managers will now have acces to always-up-to-date datas
